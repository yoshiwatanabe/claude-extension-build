# Greeting Templates

Ready-to-use greeting templates for various situations. Copy and adapt these for your specific needs.

## Professional Email Greetings

### First Contact
```
Dear [Title] [Last Name],

I hope this message finds you well. My name is [Your Name], and I am reaching out regarding [purpose].

[Body of email]

Best regards,
[Your Name]
```

### Follow-up Email
```
Hello [First Name],

Thank you for taking the time to speak with me [yesterday/last week]. I wanted to follow up on our discussion about [topic].

[Body of email]

Warm regards,
[Your Name]
```

### Cold Outreach
```
Dear [Title] [Last Name],

I hope this email finds you well. I came across [your work/your company/your article] on [topic], and I was impressed by [specific detail].

[Body of email]

Looking forward to hearing from you,
[Your Name]
```

## Meeting Greetings

### Starting a Team Meeting
```
Good morning/afternoon, everyone. Thank you all for being here. Before we dive in, I want to acknowledge [recent event/achievement]. Let's get started with [agenda item].
```

### Virtual Meeting Join
```
Good morning/afternoon! Thanks for having me. I can see [name] and [name] - great to see you all. [Wait for others to join or proceed if on time]
```

### One-on-One Meeting
```
Hi [Name], thanks for making time to meet with me today. How have you been? [Brief small talk, then:] I wanted to discuss [topic] with you.
```

## Social Greetings

### Meeting Friend's Friends
```
Hi! I'm [Your Name], [Friend's Name]'s [relationship]. Nice to meet you! How do you know [Friend's Name]?
```

### Greeting at Party
```
Hey everyone! Thanks so much for inviting me. [To host:] Your place looks amazing! [To group:] What have I missed so far?
```

### Running Into Someone
```
[Name]! Hi! How are you? It's been [timeframe] since [last meeting/event]. What have you been up to?
```

## Special Situation Greetings

### After Someone's Loss
```
Dear [Name],

I was deeply saddened to hear about [loss]. Please know that you and your family are in my thoughts during this difficult time. If there's anything I can do to support you, please don't hesitate to reach out.

With heartfelt sympathy,
[Your Name]
```

### Congratulations
```
[Name]!

Congratulations on [achievement]! I heard the news about [specific detail] and wanted to reach out personally. This is such a well-deserved accomplishment.

[Additional thoughts]

Warmest congratulations,
[Your Name]
```

```
Hi [Name],

## Video Conference Greetings

### Joining a Meeting Early
```
Hi! I'm a bit early - great to see you [Name]. How's your day going so far? [Small talk until meeting starts]
```

### Joining a Meeting On Time
```
Good morning/afternoon everyone! Sorry, just joining. Can you hear me okay? Great, please continue - I'll catch up.
```

### Starting as Host
```
Hi everyone, thank you for joining. Let's give it one more minute for everyone to get on, and then we'll get started. [Wait, then:] Great, looks like we're all here. Welcome to [meeting name].
```

## Cultural Greeting Scripts

### Japanese Business Meeting
```
[Bow slightly]
"Ohayou gozaimasu. [Your Name] to moushimasu. Douzo yoroshiku onegaishimasu."
(Good morning. My name is [Your Name]. Please treat me favorably.)
[Exchange business cards with both hands, slight bow]
```

### Arabic Business Introduction
```
"As-salamu alaykum, [Name]. Ana [Your Name]. Tasharrafna."
(Peace be upon you, [Name]. I am [Your Name]. Pleased to meet you.)
[Handshake with right hand, warm smile]
```

### Indian Formal Greeting

[Hands pressed together at chest, slight bow]

## Quick Reference by Formality

### Very Formal (Interviews, High-Level Executives, First Business Contact)
```
"Good [morning/afternoon], [Title] [Last Name]. Thank you very much for [meeting with me/your time/this opportunity]. It's an honor to [meet you/be here/speak with you]."
```

### Formal (Business Meetings, Professional Conferences, Client Meetings)
```
"Hello [Title/First Name] [Last Name], I'm [Your Name]. It's a pleasure to [meet you/see you again]. Thank you for [coming/joining us/your time]."
```

### Business Casual (Team Meetings, Regular Colleagues, Professional Network)
```
"Hi [First Name], good to see you! How are things going? [Brief small talk] Shall we get started?"
```

### Casual (Friends, Informal Gatherings, Relaxed Work Environments)
```
"Hey [Name]! How's it going? Good to see you! [Natural conversation flows]"
```

### Very Casual (Close Friends, Family, Informal Social Events)
```
"Hey! / What's up! / How are you!"
[Natural, warm, unscripted]
```

```

### Ending a Meeting
```

```

### Social Departure
```
"It was really great to see you! Let's not wait so long next time. Take care, and [relevant parting wish]."
```

### Professional Phone Call
```
"Thank you again for taking the time to speak with me today, [Name]. I appreciate your insights. Have a wonderful rest of your day."
```

## Usage Tips

1. **Personalize**: Always add specific details relevant to the person or situation
2. **Cultural awareness**: Adapt formality and style to match cultural context
3. **Relationship level**: Adjust warmth and casualness based on how well you know the person
4. **Context matters**: Same person might receive different greeting at work vs. social event
5. **Be genuine**: Templates are starting points - make them authentic to your voice
6. **Read the room**: Adjust your greeting based on the energy and mood of the situation
7. **Follow up**: Greeting sets tone - make sure your follow-through matches the warmth you start with

These templates demonstrate the range of greeting styles and provide copy-paste starting points for users learning greeting etiquette.
